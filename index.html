What is Leargon?

Leargon is an open-source enterprise platform that brings order to fragmented enterprise landscapes. It connects Data Ontologies with Business Processes, providing governance and transparency across your entire organization.

Built with a Groovy/Micronaut backend, a TypeScript/React frontend, and MySQL for persistence, Leargon is designed for modern deployment via Docker Compose or Kubernetes with Helm charts.

The Triangle: Domain, Process & Data

At the heart of Leargon lies a conceptual triangle — three interconnected pillars that together provide a complete view of your enterprise architecture.

Domain

Business Domains serve as the organizational containers — structural groupings of business capabilities. They provide the context in which processes operate and data is defined. With full versioning support, every change to a domain is tracked and auditable.

Process

Processes represent operational workflows — how work flows through the organization. Leargon maps these processes to detect bottlenecks and structural fractures before they cause impact, giving you Process Insight across your entire operation.

Data

The Data Ontology defines business entities, their relationships, and classifications. Leargon's Ontology Weaver seamlessly integrates data models into live operational workflows, ensuring your structural definitions stay connected to reality.

Together, these three pillars form an interconnected whole: Domains provide context, Processes define operational flow, and Data defines the entities and their relationships. Leargon weaves them together so organizations can see the full picture rather than working with siloed fragments.
